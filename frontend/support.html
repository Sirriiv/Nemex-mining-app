Frequently Asked Questions
How can I claim my NMX tokens?
You can claim 30 NMXp every 12 hours; this NMXp will be converted to NMX tokens.
Why is my balance not updating?
Balance updates are instant. If you are experiencing delays, please try refreshing your dashboard or checking your internet connection.
Is there a referral program?
The referral system is designed to give every user a limited amount of friends they can refer. You can share your unique link to earn NMXp.
How do I buy NMXp?
You can purchase NMXp by navigating to the "Buy NMXp" section. Select your desired package, scroll down, copy the address provided to you, send the exact amount, and wait for confirmation. Once your purchase is confirmed, your balance will update with the purchased amount.
What is the VIP token benefit?
The VIP token benefit is designed to reward users who purchase the VIP package with extra NMX tokens as a thank you. These tokens are expected to be of great value in the near future.
How secure is my account?
We use industry-standard security practices, including Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and secure authentication protocols. Your data is protected and is never shared with third parties.
