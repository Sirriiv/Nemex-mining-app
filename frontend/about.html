Nemex Ecosystem Launchpad
The Nemex Ecosystem Launchpad is the groundbreaking second pillar of the NemexCoin Ecosystem, specifically designed to confront the critical lack of trust caused by 'rug pulls' in new crypto project launches by fostering a secure, transparent, and fair environment on the TON blockchain.
Anti-Rug Pull Mechanism
The anti-rug pull mechanism works by having Nemex provide initial liquidity to vetted projects, allowing them to list and trade immediately, and then making that provided liquidity mandatory locked within a smart contract for a predefined period.
Liquidity Protection
Mandatory smart contract locking of initial liquidity prevents developers from withdrawing funds
Community Security
Fundamental protection of community interests and ensuring market stability
Value Creation
Projects contribute 5-10% of their total token supply to drive long-term value for NMX holders
